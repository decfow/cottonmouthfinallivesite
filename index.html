MISSION STATEMENT

Cotton Mouth stands as a local publication that aims to highlight and focus on the lives of creatives, musicians and organizers around the city. Throughout the last two years, we have made it our mission to implement direct action into our work, from hosting shows for benefits to writing articles on the evictions that occur in our city. We hope to actively achieve a publication that encourages citizens and artists to engage in this form of direct action.
